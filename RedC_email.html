Responsive HTML Email

Red C gave me the task of updating their emails so they would fall more in line with the new design of the website. This meant that I had to recode them all from the ground up, make them responsive and even include a "hamburger navigation menu" on email clients that would support it.

The hamburger technique is achieved using check boxes and some conditional comments for Outlook

I also recognised an opportunity to add an animation to the email just to make it a little more exciting to people who opened it
